Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 16, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how SSH Config Manager ("we", "our", or "the software") handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your data.

1. Data Collection and Usage

1.1 No Data Collection

SSH Config Manager does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information, usage data, or telemetry. Specifically:

  • No advertisements - The software contains no ads or ad trackers
  • No analytics - We do not collect analytics, crash reports, or usage statistics
  • No tracking - There are no tracking mechanisms, cookies, or user behavior monitoring
  • No data transmission - The software does not send any data to external servers during normal operation

1.2 Fully Offline Operation

The software is designed to operate completely offline. Both the trial version and the activated version can function without any internet connection. Activation itself can be completed offline by manually transferring the activation file from another internet-connected environment.

2. SSH Configuration Data

2.1 Local Storage Only

All SSH configuration data is stored exclusively on your local device in a location of your choosing. This data never leaves your device and is never uploaded to any server or third-party service.

2.2 Your Responsibility

You maintain full control and responsibility for your SSH configuration data. We recommend maintaining regular backups of your configuration files.

3. Activation and Licensing

3.1 Device Fingerprint

To verify and enforce license validity, the software generates a device fingerprint that is bound to your activation key. This fingerprint is generated locally on your device and is only transmitted during the activation process if you choose to use online activation.

3.2 Activation Data

When you activate the software (whether online or offline), the following information may be processed:

  • Device fingerprint (hardware-based identifier)
  • License key
  • Activation status

This information is used solely for license validation purposes.

3.3 Data Deletion Rights

You have the right to request:

  • License unbinding: Contact us via email to unbind your license from a device, allowing activation on a different device
  • Complete data deletion: Request the complete deletion of all your personal activation-related data from our records

4. Third-Party Services

SSH Config Manager does not integrate with or rely on any third-party services, APIs, or libraries that collect user data. The software operates independently on your device.

5. Children's Privacy

Our software is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. Continued use of the software after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
